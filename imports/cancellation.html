01

Introduction & Scope

At Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort, we understand that travel plans can change. Because our remote island location on Mfangano requires careful preparation for every guest's arrival, we have established this policy to balance flexibility for our guests with the operational realities of our small, family-run property.

This policy applies to all reservations made directly with our management team by telephone. By confirming your booking, you agree to these cancellation terms.

Our Promise: We strive to be as accommodating as possible. If your plans change, please communicate with us as early as you can so we can assist you in the best way possible.

02

How to Cancel Your Reservation

Since all reservations at Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort are handled directly by our management team via telephone, all cancellations must also be made by telephone to ensure immediate confirmation and proper processing.

  • Primary Line: +254 756 585 016
  • Alternative Line: +254 715 249 204

You may follow up with an email to paulnyumbwe@gmail.com for your personal records, but the cancellation is only considered official once verbally confirmed by our management team.

warning Important Notice

Email-only cancellation requests may not be processed in time to avoid late cancellation fees. Please always call us directly to cancel your booking.

03

Cancellation Timeline & Fees

Refund eligibility is determined by the amount of notice provided before your scheduled check-in date. The following timeline applies to all standard reservations:

Notice Period Refund Amount Details
7 days or more before check-in 100% Refund Full refund of any deposit or payment made.
3 to 6 days before check-in 50% Refund 50% refund of the first night's rate.
Less than 3 days before check-in No Refund 100% of the first night's rate is retained.

Peak Season Notice

During our peak seasons (December–March and June–October), we kindly request 14 days' notice for a full refund due to high demand and limited room availability on the island.

04

Modifying Your Stay

We are happy to help you change your travel dates or room type if your plans shift. All modifications must be requested by telephone and are subject to the following conditions:

  • Date Changes: Subject to room availability on your new dates. Any rate differences will be applied.
  • Room Changes: Upgrades are subject to availability. Downgrades may be treated as a partial cancellation under our standard timeline.
  • Guest Count: Adding guests may require a rate adjustment. Reducing guest count follows the standard cancellation policy for the removed portion.

Modifications requested within 3 days of your original arrival date may be treated as a cancellation and a new booking under our standard policy.

05

No-Show Policy

If you do not arrive on your scheduled check-in date and have not contacted us to cancel or modify your reservation, the booking is considered a "No-Show."

  • The full first night's rate will be charged and is non-refundable.
  • All remaining nights in the reservation will be automatically cancelled.
  • Future bookings may require full prepayment at the time of reservation.

Travel Delays: If you are delayed due to ferry schedules, weather, or transport issues, please call us as soon as possible. We may be able to hold your room or adjust your dates on a case-by-case basis.

06

Exceptions & Force Majeure

We recognise that travelling to Mfangano Island involves ferry crossings and can be affected by circumstances beyond anyone's control. We may waive standard cancellation fees in exceptional situations, including:

  • Ferry Cancellations: If the Sena Ferry is cancelled or suspended due to weather or mechanical issues.
  • Severe Weather: Documented severe weather preventing safe travel to the island.
  • Medical Emergencies: Documented medical emergencies preventing travel (physician's note required).
  • Government Restrictions: Official travel restrictions or border closures.

In these cases, we will work directly with you to reschedule your stay to a future date without penalty, or provide a full refund if rescheduling is not possible.

07

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Since we accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and mobile money, refunds are handled as follows:

  • M-Pesa / Mobile Money: Processed within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Bank Transfer: Processed within 7 to 14 business days, depending on your bank's processing times.

You will receive a confirmation message or email once the refund has been initiated by our management team.
