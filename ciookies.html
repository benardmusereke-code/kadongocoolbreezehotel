    1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your actions and preferences (like login, language, font size) over time so you don't have to re-enter them.

    Similar technologies we may use include:

    • Local Storage: Stores data persistently on your device for app functionality
    • Web Beacons: Tiny images that track page views or email opens
    • Device Fingerprinting: Collects device characteristics for security and fraud prevention

    2. Why We Use Cookies

    Cookies enable Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort to:

    • Keep you logged in to your account and booking portal
    • Remember your language, currency, and accessibility preferences
    • Analyze website traffic to improve user experience and content
    • Personalize offers and recommendations based on your interests
    • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
    • Ensure website security and prevent fraudulent activity

    3. Types of Cookies We Use

    Cookie Category Purpose Duration Managed By
    Essential Enable core website functions: secure login, booking engine, session management Session to 1 year Kadongo Resort
    Analytics Understand how visitors use our site (pages viewed, time spent) via Google Analytics Up to 2 years Google LLC
    Marketing Deliver personalized ads on social media and search engines; measure campaign performance Up to 13 months Meta, Google
    Preferences Remember your choices: language, region, accessibility settings 1 year Kadongo Resort

    Important: Essential cookies cannot be disabled. They are strictly necessary for the website to function and are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services.

    4. Third-Party Cookies

    We partner with trusted service providers who may place cookies on your device:

    • Google Analytics: For website usage analytics
    • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): For ad measurement and retargeting
    • Booking Engine Provider: For reservation functionality and payment processing
    • Cloudflare: For security, performance, and DDoS protection

    These parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

    5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

    Browser Settings

    You can control cookies through your browser settings:

    • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
    • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
    • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
    • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

    Note: Disabling essential cookies may break website functionality like booking or login.

    Opt-Out of Marketing Cookies

    Manage personalized ads via industry opt-out tools:

    • Google Ads: adssettings.google.com
    • Meta: Facebook Ad Preferences
    • Global: aboutads.info/choices

    6. Do Not Track Signals

    Some browsers send "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. While there is no universal standard for responding to DNT, Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort respects your privacy choices. Our analytics and marketing partners honor industry opt-out mechanisms where available.
