Getting Here

Your Path to Mfangano Island

Mfangano Island is accessible only by boat across Lake Victoria. Here are the most common ways travellers reach us from mainland Kenya.

flight
Option 1 · Fastest

Fly to Kisumu, Then Travel to the Lake

International and domestic flights arrive at Kisumu International Airport (KIS). From Kisumu, you have two choices: take a short connecting flight to Mbita Airstrip, or continue by road to Homa Bay or Mbita.

Airlines such as Safarilink and Airkenya Express operate regular routes to the Lake Victoria region.

directions_car
Option 2 · Scenic Drive

Road Journey to Mbita or Homa Bay

From Kisumu, it is approximately a 2 to 3 hour drive through the beautiful countryside of western Kenya to either Mbita or Homa Bay town. The route follows the A109 and A1 highways, offering lovely views of the highlands descending toward the lake.

Private car, hired taxi, or regular matatu (minibus) services are all available. We can provide further guidance upon request.

directions_boat
Option 3 · The Lake Crossing

Ferry from Mbita to Sena, Mfangano Island

From Mbita, the regular passenger and vehicle ferry crosses to Sena landing point on Mfangano Island. The crossing takes approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour and runs on a published schedule throughout the day.

Smaller private boats are also available for hire at Mbita jetty if you prefer a more direct crossing or arrive outside ferry hours.

Helpful Tips

Travel Wisely

A few practical notes to help you plan your journey to Mfangano Island with confidence.

schedule

Ferry Schedules

The Mbita–Sena ferry runs on a regular schedule throughout the day. We recommend checking current times in advance and arriving early during peak seasons.

payments

Carry Some Cash

While the resort accepts various payment methods, it is wise to carry Kenyan Shillings (KES) for ferry tickets, local markets, and small purchases on the island.

signal_cellular_alt

Mobile Coverage

Mobile network coverage on Mfangano Island is available but can be limited in certain areas. Complimentary Wi-Fi is provided at the resort for all guests.

luggage

Pack Light, Pack Smart

Comfortable clothing, sunscreen, insect repellent, a light jacket for evenings by the lake, and good walking shoes for exploring the island's trails and sites.
